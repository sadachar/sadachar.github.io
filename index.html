Our Mission

"Improve elderly online participation in a world that is becoming increasingly digital."

There's a problem within our communities.
The digital divide.

There is a disparity, a ‘generation gap’, in how the youth and elderly use technology. While teenagers regularly use technology to connect with friends, senior citizens find technology daunting to learn and stray away from it. But, nowhere is this issue more prevalent than in senior homes. where family visitors come to meet the elderly only a few times a year– leaving the residents feeling disconnected from their families. While their problem could easily be solved by video calling their grandchildren, emailing relatives, or viewing loved ones’ photos on Facebook, most residents don't regularly use computers or have services to teach them how to utilize technology for their benefit.
